Gulmarg - For The Adventure Enthusiasts

Gulmarg is one of the most popular valleys in Kashmir. Offering breathtaking beauty, the valley used to be the most preferred recreational spot for Mughal Emperor Jahangir. The valley has now developed into a pivotal location spot for gondolas and skiing. It hosts two major skiing events each year, during the festivities of Easter and Christmas.
Nearby Attractions:
1.Pahalgam
2.Sonamarg
3.Leh


Dha Hanu Valley – Land Of Aryans

The valley of Dha Hanu is the popular land of Aryans which showcases a diverse cultural harmony amidst the natural surroundings. Beautifully surrounded by the villages of Ladakh, the Dha Hanu Valley is located at a lower altitude which makes it a preferred place among visitors to spend a quaint and peaceful time. Dha Hanu Valley is formed by Hindukush Mountains in addition to the river Indus and essentially attracts visitors from each and every part of the world.
Nearby Attractions:
1.Moon Land Of Lamayuru
2.Chadar Trek
3.St. Thomas Church


Sindh Valley – The Historical Land

Singh Valley has proven to be a significant aspect of the silk route. It also worked as a bridge between China and India. The route is the entrance of Islam and Buddhism and is popular for the dense pine forests and alpine meadows. The valley is popular for being a major source of hydroelectric power to the state and offers water for irrigation. Tourists can also spot Ibex and Markhor wild animals in the valley.
Nearby Attractions:
1.Vale Of Kashmir
2.Betaab Valley
3.Sind River
